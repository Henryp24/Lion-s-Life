Scarface

Summary

Meet a living legend, Scarface. His coalition, the Musketeers, protect the Paradise Pride … the most lethal pride in the Mara. ,
Their specialty is picking off tired animals as they cross the Great Mara River.
A male lion coalition roams their territory, mating and protecting one or two prides.
Yet Scar has definitely shown preference to the Paradise Pride.
They’re a family: nine females, nine cubs, and six young males.
Adulthood isn’t guaranteed for all of them And those that make it will surely develop scars and stories of their own..

Three cool facts about Lions:

  1. A lion may sleep up to 20 hours a day.
  2. A lion’s heels don’t touch the ground when it walks.
  3. A lion’s roar can be heard from as far as 5 miles away.
